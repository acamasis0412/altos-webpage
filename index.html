FAQ

Questions, answered.

What does Altos do?

Altos is a full-stack software studio. We build custom software of all kinds — web apps, internal tools, and complete systems — and we specialize in integrations that connect your existing tools so they work as one.

What integrations does Altos specialize in?

We connect e-commerce and webstore APIs, inventory systems, payment providers, and third-party tools. If a system has an API, we can wire it into the rest of your stack and keep data flowing in real time.

Which platforms and systems can Altos integrate?

Common ones include Alibaba, Etsy, freewebstore, Shopify, and WooCommerce, plus inventory and order-management systems. Because we build to each API, we can integrate virtually any platform that exposes one.

How fast can Altos deliver?

We're AI-accelerated and senior-led, so we typically ship in days or weeks rather than months — without cutting corners. Every output is reviewed and owned by senior engineers.

Is Altos expensive?

No. A lean, globally distributed senior team lets us deliver top-tier engineering without a top-tier agency price tag — premium quality at a fair, efficient rate.

What is ShopLink?

ShopLink is Altos's own inventory product. It connects your webstores, syncs stock in real time across every channel, unifies orders in one place, and turns it all into reports. It is the clearest proof of the integration work we do for clients. Explore ShopLink.
